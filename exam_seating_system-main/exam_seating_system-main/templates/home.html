EXAM HALL SEAT ALLOCATION SYSTEM

The purpose of developing exam hall seating arrangement system is to computerize the traditional way of conducting exams and help staff allocate exam halls easily without any burden. Another purpose of developing this software is to generate the report automatically during exams at the end of the session or in between the session.
